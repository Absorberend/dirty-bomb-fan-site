What is Dirty Bomb?

Dirty Bomb is a team based free to play first person shooter game where a fierce battle unravels in the post-apocalyptic wasteland of London. With no law and/or order, it's time to call in the mercenaries. Suit up and go toe-to-toe with other soldiers of fortune as you battle it out for riches and bragging rights.

Aimee is by far my most favorite merc (character). She's amazing at quick peeking your enemies and annoying them. You can hold down sites with her ability which deals an additional 15% damage to enemies. OP!
Vassili is my 2nd best merc. He's a bit slower than Aimee, but he does have more health. His ability is basically a wallhack. Sounds OP right? Well, it kinda is! You can anticipate your enemies and instantly headshot them whenever they show themselves.
If both Aimee and Vassili are banned I usually play Redeye. He doesn't have the Felix sniper-rifle (the weapon I really enjoy), but he's still an alright sniper. If you're good with the Grandeur you'll be stomping in pugs!
